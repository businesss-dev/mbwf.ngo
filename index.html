About Us

At Maa Bhagwati Welfare Foundation, we are dedicated to building sustainable change through healthcare access, educational upliftment, environmental care, and women empowerment programs across underserved communities.

Our purpose

To serve with dignity, strength, and cultural pride while creating measurable impact in rural and urban India.
